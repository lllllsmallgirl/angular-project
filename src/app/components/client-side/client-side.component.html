XiJing Hospital
Xijing Hospital is located in the beautiful ancient capital of Xi'an, and is the first affiliated hospital of the Fourth Military Medical University. The hospital always adheres to the "mission culture" of practicing its mission and saving lives and injuries, the "innovative culture" of being brave to compete and constantly surpass, the "boutique culture" of striving for excellence, the "sunshine culture" of fair competition and harmonious development, and is determined to forge ahead and innovate. It has successively won the National Top 100 Hospitals, National People's Rest assured Demonstration Hospital Honored as a national model unit for supporting the government and loving the people, an advanced division and brigade unit in the rear, and an advanced party committee in the rear. In the national hospital comprehensive ranking released by authoritative domestic departments, the hospital's comprehensive strength ranks among the top five in the country, and the hospital name is recognized as a well-known trademark in China by the State Administration for Industry and Commerce.

Service

Xijing Hospital has a comprehensive medical service system, professional medical staff, and advanced medical equipment, which can ensure that patients receive high-quality treatment and nursing services.

Health Promotion

Xijing Hospital will continue to uphold the health concept of serving the military and civilians, and carry out more forms of innovative science popularization activities around health issues of general concern to the public, to protect the health of the people.
